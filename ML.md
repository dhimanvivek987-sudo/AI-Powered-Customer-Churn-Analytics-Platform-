# Machine Learning Model

Model: Logistic Regression

## Data Preparation

- Converted numeric fields
- One-hot encoded geography & gender
- Train/Test split: 80/20

## Performance

Accuracy: 0.815  
ROC-AUC: 0.734  

## Business Interpretation

Higher churn probability associated with:

- Inactive membership
- Lower credit score
- Certain geographic segments
- High balance customers with low engagement
